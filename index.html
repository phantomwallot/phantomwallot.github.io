Security Measures

In the realm of cryptocurrency, security is non-negotiable. Phantom Wallet places a strong emphasis on safeguarding users' assets through a combination of robust security measures. The wallet is non-custodial, meaning users have complete control over their private keys, adding an extra layer of security by reducing the risk of centralized vulnerabilities. Multi-Signature Support

Phantom Wallet recognizes that some users may prefer an extra layer of security for their digital assets. To accommodate this, the wallet offers multi-signature support. Multi-signature, or multisig, requires multiple private keys to authorize a transaction, reducing the risk of unauthorized access. This feature provides users with greater control and flexibility over the security of their funds.
